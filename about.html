About Eagle I Industrial

Eagle Industrial Electric, Inc. is an electrical contractor serving the City of Philadelphia and surrounding areas. Specializing in commercial building installations, high voltage cabling and substation construction, emergency generation installations, aviation projects, antenna & wireless systems installation, building systems installation, lighting retrofits, restaurant/retail fit outs, and complete electrical systems maintenance. We offer 24-hour emergency service for any/all electrical situations that may arise.

Eagle Industrial Electric, Inc. performs all our electrical construction and maintenance projects with fully trained and skilled Local 98 Electrical Union Project Foreman, Journeyman, and Apprentices. With the cooperation from Local 98 we are able to provide our customers with the best-trained and dedicated workman available in our industry. This, combined with our skilled management staff, will reflect on our ability to satisfy any requirements that may be needed for construction and maintenance projects of any size.

Eagle I. Electric Inc has been in business since 1988. With offices in Essington, PA as well as South Philadelphia we are placed local and readily available to be able to serve our customers in the Philadelphia International Airport and throughout the city.
